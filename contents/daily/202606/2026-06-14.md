## Stanford CS230 | Autumn 2025 | Lecture 8: Agents, Prompts, and RAG

Why do we need to augment LLMs
- LLMs are difficult to control
- The LLM may underperform in your task
- context windows are limited

Chain of Thought
- 把任務拆成Step1,2,3,...,不要跳過任何步驟

Zero shot / Few shot prompting

Challenges with standalone LLMs
1. Limited context windows
2. Knowledge gaps
3. Hallucinations
4. Lack of sources

RAG, Retrieval-Augmented Generation（檢索增強生成）
先找資料（Retrieval），再生成答案（Generation）。

文件 → 切塊 (Chunking) → Embedding → 向量資料庫(Vector DB) → 相似度搜尋 → 取回相關內容 → LLM 生成答案
